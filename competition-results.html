Annual SWAT Competition Results

February 20, 2024

By Sergeant Emily Thompson

The Dallas County SWAT team achieved outstanding results at the 2024 Regional SWAT Competition, securing first place in multiple categories and demonstrating exceptional tactical skills. The competition, which featured teams from across the state, tested participants in various tactical scenarios and physical challenges.

Competition Highlights

The team excelled in several key areas:

  • First place in Tactical Team Challenge
  • Top marks in Marksmanship Competition
  • Best performance in Hostage Rescue Scenario
  • Outstanding achievement in Physical Fitness Test

Team Performance

Team members demonstrated exceptional skill and teamwork throughout the competition. The rigorous training program implemented in the months leading up to the event clearly paid off, with the team showing marked improvement in all competition areas compared to previous years.

"Our team's performance at this year's competition reflects their dedication to excellence and continuous improvement. These results are a testament to the hard work and commitment of every team member."
- Lieutenant Michael Brown, Team Leader

Looking Ahead

Building on this success, the team is now preparing for the National SWAT Competition scheduled for later this year. The department has implemented additional training modules to further enhance the team's capabilities in preparation for the national stage.
